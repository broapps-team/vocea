# Privacy Policy for Vocea

**Effective Date:** March 29, 2026
**Contact:** [broapps.team@gmail.com](mailto:broapps.team@gmail.com)

Vocea is a personal memory companion app that allows users to capture their thoughts, moments, and ideas through voice or text, in their native language, and retrieve them later just by asking.

---

## What We Collect
- We **do not collect** personal data from your device or account.
- Your memories are stored **locally on your device** (and/or in your iCloud/backup if you enable it via system settings).
- We collect AI usage data for tracking the tokens and credits used by the app.
- We do **not** use third-party advertising SDKs or track you across apps or websites.

## Children’s Privacy
The app is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided us personal information, contact us at broapps.team@gmail.com to request deletion.

## Data Security
We use industry-standard measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## Your Choices
You can delete the app to remove on-device data. (If you enabled iCloud/backup, data may persist in your Apple backups per your iCloud settings.)

## Changes
We may update this policy from time to time. We’ll post updates here and revise the Effective Date.
